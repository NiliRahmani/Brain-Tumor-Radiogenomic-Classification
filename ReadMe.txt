Brain Tumor Radiogenomic Classification
Predict the status of a genetic biomarker important for brain cancer treatment
the task is to predict the genetic subtype of glioblastoma using MRI (magnetic resonance imaging) scans to train and test the model to detect for the presence of MGMT promoter methylation.
